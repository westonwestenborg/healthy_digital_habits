Digital Wellness Basics

New to digital wellness? Start here with the fundamentals every parent should know.

Screen Time Guidelines

Ages 2-5: 1 hour of high-quality content
Ages 6+: Consistent limits that don't interfere with sleep, physical activity, or other healthy behaviors
Teens: Focus on content quality and time balance rather than strict limits

Warning Signs to Watch

  • Sleep disruption
  • Mood changes around screen time
  • Decline in grades or activities
  • Social isolation
  • Physical complaints

First Steps for Parents

  1. Track current screen time for one week
  2. Create screen-free zones (bedrooms, dining table)
  3. Establish a family media plan
  4. Model healthy digital habits
  5. Plan alternative activities
