Screen Time & Sleep Tracking Tools

üïí Screen Time Tracker

A simple daily or weekly log to track time spent on different activities

Track time spent on:

  • Social media
  • Gaming
  • Watching videos
  • Schoolwork or learning apps

How to use it:

  1. Use the tracker for a few days to notice patterns
  2. Talk about what feels "just right" vs "too much"
  3. Try one small change (like no screens for 30 mins before bed)

üåô Sleep Tracker

A basic chart to track bedtime, wake time, and total sleep each night

Tips for Better Sleep:

  • Stick to consistent bedtime and wake time, even on weekends
  • Avoid screens 30-60 mins before bedtime
  • Create a relaxing bedtime routine (reading, deep breathing, listening to calming music)

Tips for Families:

  • Make it a no-pressure conversation
  • Celebrate small wins, like getting an extra 30 mins of rest
  • Consider a family "screen break" time where everyone puts devices away together
