Tips for Success

Start Small

Pick one or two changes to focus on first. Success with small changes builds momentum for bigger ones.

Be Consistent

It's better to enforce simpler rules consistently than complex rules sporadically.

Include Your Child

When age-appropriate, involve them in creating the plan. They're more likely to follow rules they helped create.

Track Progress

Use our tracking tools to monitor improvements and celebrate small wins along the way.
